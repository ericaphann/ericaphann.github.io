What is FIRST Robotics?

FIRST Robotics, also known as For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology, is a robotics competition that allows students to show their creative minds in creating robots to achieve different goals and objectives. It's a global competition to help inspire students to spark creativity and innovation. Different schools can participate and compete against each other, gaining valuable experience in business, technology, and teamwork

FIRST has multiple different competitions for high school and middle school students, FIRST Tech Challenge, FIRST Robotics, and FIRST Lego League. Each competition has their own goals, challenges and objectives.

About My Team!

My robotics team is Avian Robotics 9005. It was founded in 2022 at East Mecklenburg High School. We were a team of around 10-15 people with a mix of juniors and seniors, and only 3 girls. We focused on challenging ourselves to new things and learning. As a rookie team we were new to everything and got mentoring and help from our mentor team Yeti Robotics.
