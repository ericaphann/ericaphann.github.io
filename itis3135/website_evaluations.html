Website Evaluations

Good Website Design: www.csszengarden.com/220/

Website that was created to give inspiration for different css designs

  • Contrast: Basic colors used, that are easy on the eye and consistently used throughout the website
  • Repetition: The background stays the same and the same color scheme is repeated
  • Alignment: The links are aligned on the left hand side, making it convinient and visually appealing
  • Proximity: The texts are grouped into different chuncks based on the topics and ideas

Bad Website Design: www.yyyyyyy.info/

Really really bad website that was used to show users an example of a bad website

  • Contrast: WAYY too many colors and images everywhere
  • Repetition: There is no type of style being repeated or used
  • Alignment: There is no alignment of text, images, or anything, all of it is on different alignments
  • Proximity: No groupings of items, different images and texts are randomly placed
